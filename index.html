Eggs are a great breakfast food and take minimal preperation. Unfortunatley no, you can not toast an egg.

Pizza

While unable to be made inside of a toaster, pizza may be toasted if you so desire.

French Fries

If you're in the mood for soggy, burnt fries, then putting them in the toaster is the move for you. Yes, you can toast that.

Whole Avocado

Avocado toast's wild cousin, toasted avocado makes a great treat for any get together. Yes, you can toast that, just make sure you jam it into the toaster correctly.
